# Tweet: ERC-8004 Identity

Autonomous agents need onchain identity too.

We're implementing ERC-8004 for Bitcoin Yield Copilot:
- Every transaction signed by the agent's verifiable identity
- Reputation accumulated over time
- Users know exactly who (or what) is executing their trades

No anonymous transactions. No hidden principals. Just transparent, accountable AI agent operations on the Stacks blockchain.

The future of DeFi isn't just about trustless contracts - it's about verifiable agent identities.
